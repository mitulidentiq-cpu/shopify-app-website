AI Studio

The AI Studio is a full-featured suite of 13 AI-powered tools built directly into the app, powered by Google Gemini 2.5 Flash (for text, vision, and code) and Groq models (llama-3.3-70b-versatile and llama-4-scout-17b) for rapid processing.

All AI results can be saved to local history (15 entries per tool) and restored at any time.

7. Color Palette Generator

8. FAQ Generator

9. Product Description Writer

Getting Started Guide

Step 1: Install the App

Install the app from the Shopify App Store on your merchant dashboard.

Step 2: Enable App Embed

Enable the App Embed within the Shopify Theme Customizer:

  1. Click the "Enable App Embed" link in the app dashboard.
  2. In your Shopify theme settings editor, navigate to the App Embeds tab.
  3. Toggle the AI Section Hub embed to ON.
  4. Click Save in the top right.

Step 3: Browse and Install Sections

Navigate to Explore Sections, preview the templates, and click Add to Theme to inject them.

Step 4: Place Section on Page

Inside the Theme Editor, navigate to the page (e.g. Home, Product page), click Add Section in the sidebar, find your section under the Apps dropdown, and drag it to your desired location.

Frequently Asked Questions

Sections & Installation

Will sections slow down my store?
No. The sections are native Liquid templates served via Shopify CDN. They load without external Javascript runtimes and have zero impact on PageSpeed scores.

Can I use sections on multiple pages?
Yes. You can add any installed section template to multiple pages, and customize settings independently on each page template.

Can I customize section source code?
Yes. Sections are written directly into your theme's code under the Sections directory, and can be edited via Online Store > Edit Code.

AI Studio

Is my customer data shared with AI models?
No. Only store metadata (such as product name, theme colors) is shared to generate tailored designs or copy. No sensitive customer data is accessed.

Instagram Reels

Does this app require official Instagram API approval?
No. The app uses an advanced multi-fallback public scraping technique, eliminating the need to set up Facebook developer applications or request access.

Klenzo: AI Variants — User Guide

Welcome to the official user guide! We are thrilled to have you here.

Our app is designed to elevate your store by seamlessly transforming standard product variant dropdowns into stunning, clickable visual swatches. By providing your customers with an intuitive, highly visual way to browse product options directly on your product pages, you'll instantly upgrade their shopping experience and drive stronger engagement.

Quick Start (5 Minutes)

Want to get up and running immediately? Follow these fast steps:

  1. Install the app from the Shopify App Store.
  2. Open the app in your Shopify Admin.
  3. Select a Swatch Style (choose from beautiful Circle Swatches, detailed Image Cards, or classic Image Swatches).
  4. Save your settings.
  5. Enable the App Embed in your Theme Editor and click Save.
  6. Open a product page on your storefront to see your new swatches!

Done!

Before You Start

To ensure everything works perfectly, please check the following requirements:

Pre-requisites

  • Theme App Embed Must Be Enabled: The swatches will not display until you explicitly enable the App Embed block inside your Theme Customizer.
  • Products Must Have Variants: Swatches are only generated for products that have variants (e.g., Size, Color, Material).
  • Variant Images Must Be Assigned: The app relies on your existing store data. Ensure you have attached images to your variants inside the standard Product Admin.

We offer powerful customization features designed specifically for modern Shopify merchants:

Enable Theme Extension

To make the swatches visible to your customers, you must enable the App Embed in your Theme Editor.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. From your Admin, go to Online Store → Themes.
  2. Find your current Live Theme and click Customize.
  3. In the left sidebar of the Theme Editor, click on the App embeds icon (it looks like a block of squares).
  4. Find the app's embed block in the list.
  5. Toggle the switch to Enable (it will turn green).
  6. Click Save in the top right corner of the Theme Editor.

Swatch Configuration & Image Mapping

Our system takes a streamlined approach to swatch generation.

Automatic Variant Images

The app automatically generates your swatches using the images you have already assigned to your variants in the standard Product editor. You do not need to manually upload colors or images inside the app!

  1. Go to your Shopify Admin > Products.
  2. Select a product.
  3. In the Variants section, ensure every variant has an image attached to it.
  4. The app will automatically pull these images and convert them into beautiful swatches on your storefront based on your chosen Swatch Style.

Updating Existing Swatches

When you update product variants or variant images, the app will use the latest product data. If the changes are not immediately visible, refresh the product page or clear your browser cache. No additional setup inside the app is required.

Display Styles & Settings

You can customize the appearance of your swatches by navigating to Settings inside the dashboard.

1. Swatch Styles

  • Circle: Soft, modern rounded swatches.
  • Image Cards: Large format cards that display the variant image prominently.
  • Image Swatches: Standard rectangular thumbnail swatches.

2. Display Scopes

Control exactly which products get swatches:

  • All Products: Enable across your entire store catalog.
  • Selected Products: Only show swatches on specific products you choose.
  • All Collections: Enable for all products within any collection.
  • Selected Collections: Only show swatches for products within specific collections.

3. Image Card Settings

If you choose the "Image Cards" style, you have advanced customization options:

  • Show Pricing: Toggle whether the variant price is displayed inside the card.
  • Show Badges: Enable custom badges on the cards.
  • Badge Text: Type custom text like "SALE" or "HOT".
  • Badge Styling: Use the color pickers to change the Background, Text Color, and Border. Adjust the Font Weight and Radius.

4. Out-of-Stock Styling

Define how unavailable variants appear to customers:

  • Dark Overlay
  • Grayscale + Ribbon
  • Blur + Center Badge
  • Bottom Banner

Common Setup Mistakes

Running into an issue? Check these frequent setup mistakes before contacting support:

App Embed is Disabled

The #1 reason swatches do not appear is that the App Embed is toggled off in the Theme Editor. Always ensure it is enabled and saved.

Missing Variant Images

The app relies entirely on the variant images you set in your store. If a variant does not have an image attached in the Product Admin, a swatch cannot be generated.

Product Outside Display Scope

If you selected "Selected Products" or "Selected Collections" in the Display Scope settings, ensure the product you are viewing is actually included in that scope.

Theme Cache Issues

If you just changed a setting but don't see it on your live store, it is likely a browser caching issue. Try opening your product page in an Incognito/Private window.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Do I need to manually upload color hex codes?

No. The app uses your existing variant images to generate swatches automatically, saving you hours of manual data entry.

2. What happens if I uninstall the app?

Because it uses App Embeds, uninstalling leaves no messy code in your theme. Your store will cleanly revert to standard dropdowns.

3. Do I need to know how to code to use this?

Not at all! Everything is managed through the visual settings dashboard and the Theme Editor.

4. How do I change my swatch shapes and sizes?

Navigate to Settings. You can select your core Swatch Style (Circle, Image Swatch, Image Card), toggle between circle and square shapes, and adjust pixel dimensions.

5. Can I choose which products show swatches?

Yes! In the Settings, use the Display Scope section to apply swatches to your entire catalog, specific products, or specific collections.

6. Can I add a custom "SALE" badge to my swatches?

Yes. If you use the "Image Cards" style, you can enable Badges and type your custom badge text directly in the settings. You can also customize the background color, text color, and border.

7. How do I enable Hover Preview and Dynamic Titles?

Navigate to Settings and toggle "Hover Preview" and "Dynamic Titles" on. The product title will now update automatically when a variant is selected.

8. What Out-of-Stock styles do you support?

You can choose from Dark Overlay, Grayscale + Ribbon, Blur + Center Badge, or a Bottom Banner. Currently, unavailable swatches are styled to make their status clear, but are not hidden completely.

9. What if a variant doesn't have an image?

If a variant has no image assigned in your store's admin, the app cannot generate a swatch for it. Ensure all variants have images.

10. What if my theme already has built-in swatches?

We recommend disabling your theme's native swatches in your Theme Editor to prevent overlapping UI elements. If duplicate swatches appear, disable your theme's native swatches and keep the Klenzo App Embed enabled.
